Mulling Your Own Paint

Mulling is the process of incorporating pigment particles into a vehicle or medium using a glass muller and slab. The process of mulling breaks up agglomerates of pigment particles.. By breaking the particles apart, it allows a greater surface area of particles to be thoroughly saturated (or wetted) by the medium. The result is a more fully incorporated pigment. The process of mulling should not be confused with grinding done in a mortar and pestle which grinds the particles into smaller pieces.

Why Should You Mull Your Own Paint:

Making your own paints can have several benefits. They include control of your ingredients, creating colors not found in ready-made paints, cost savings, and control over the specific working quality of a paint. In addition, many find mulling their own colors to be very satisfying. People even report that the process of mulling can have a meditative quality.
